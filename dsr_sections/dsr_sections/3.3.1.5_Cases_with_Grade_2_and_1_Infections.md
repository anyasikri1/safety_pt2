3.3.1.5
Cases with Grade 2 and 1 Infections
A total of 73 cases with 78 AEs (along with 1 Grade 5 AE and 2 Grade 4 AEs18)
reporting Grade 1 and 2 infections were reported from the Company Safety Database.
An overview of these cases is provided below.
Among these 73 cases, majority (65/73) were from Clinical Study, were medically
confirmed (49/73) and included an indication for lung cancer (42/73). Among these 73
cases, 37 concerned males and the remaining concerned female patients. The age
ranged from 26 to 87 years.
Among the AEs, Pneumonia was the most commonly reported PT (20/78), followed by
COVID 19 (5/78), Urinary tract infection and Gastroenteritis (each 4/78). The first dose
latency for the AEs ranged from 4 to 1094 days.
These cases are not discussed further for any risk factor analysis as per strategy
provided in Section 2.3.1. However, the case listings are provided in Appendix 8.
